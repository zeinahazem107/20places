About Us

"Travel Guide" is a website mainly created to help guide tourists and travelers through their journeys around the world. Its main purpose is to provide people with a simple list that will facilitate the process of selecting places to visit for vacation by reviewing some of the most visited places around the world; ranging from places such as Italy, Egypt, and France, all the way to places like Bali, Germany, Thailand, and more!
